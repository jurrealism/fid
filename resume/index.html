Sarah J. Khan - Freelance Artist

Home

Sarah J. Khan is a freelance artist through both Traditional 2D art and Digital Media.

She specializes in pet portraiture and other realistic art commissions, using different media such as acrylic paints, charcoal, colored chalk pastels, and graphite. Also, has Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator experience by making posters, social media posts, and other graphics for the Goldsboro Museum in Sanford, FL.

Received her high school diploma from the Osceola County School for the Arts in May 2017, and is currently working on finishing her A.A. in Visual Arts and Emerging Media at Seminole State College of Florida.
